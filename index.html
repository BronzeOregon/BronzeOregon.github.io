Welcome!

Red Goddess Oracle was founded with the goal of providing metaphysical readings and spiritual guidance to people of all walks of life, providing assistance in resolving lingering doubts and questions.

What is a metaphysical reading?

A metaphysical reading is wherein messages are channeled from spirit through the use of abilities, sensitivities and tools, such as pendulums, tarot, and runes.

What's the difference between metaphysical and psychic readings?

Psychic readings generally don't utilize any tools, instead directly using a combination of "clairs", spirit teams, and claircognizance, which differs from other clairs in that it shows possible future outcomes.

Mediumship readings on the other hand, focus on connecting with the departed, such as loved ones that have passed on, and are recognized as being distinct from psychic readings. These focus on answering questions, passing messages, and sharing the gift of love that transcends mortal boundaries.
