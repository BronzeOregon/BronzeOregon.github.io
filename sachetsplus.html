Sachets

Sachets have been a part of metaphysical and spiritual rituals and arts for centuries. Commonly comprised of herbs, roots, flowers and oils along with a base powder, these can be used to dust paperwork, sprinkle on candles, or ward your home.

Candles

Spell and prayer candles are prepared primarily with mind to the intent of the user. Selecting the correct candle is a matter of determining the appropriate color, herbs, anointing oils, etchings/writings and more based upon the end goal. Typically, I use candles for my altars and specific rituals, such as a cord-cutting ritual.

Stones

Various stones and crystals are used as foci when working in the metaphysical realm. It is believed that many can resonate with individuals on a personal level, and while there are too many stones to go over in detail here, we offer a wide variety including jewelry with stones and crystals set in them for everyday wear.
